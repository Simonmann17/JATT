# JATT
JATT - Job Application Tracking and Telemetry

Welcome to the JATT App! Your one stop shop for tracking all those pesky job applications on a million different sites.

This is a open source, completely free to use application that allows you to track job applications from various vendors. As you may know (unfortunately)
certain vendors (Workday, Brassrings, etc.) cause you to make a new account every time you apply to a different domain under the same vendor 
and due to the fact that attempting to centralize all these various accounts is apparently a T&A violation (I hate paperwork), the JATT app was born. This stellar 
application allows you to view not only job applications from a vendor and specific company, but also application status and metrics.

This project parses your email (with your permission of course) using Gmail's very convenient API and extracts job application notifications from vendors both specific and general. We do not store
any personal information from either Google account or email (we respectfully do not care enough to do so), nor will it save or extract links, ensuring full security of your 
data and applications.

We allow users to save and discard specific notifications from employers (no, Steven I am not interested in becoming a part-time piano instructor) for you the user to maintain a clean and easy to read dashboard.
Job tracking is already a pain and we don't want your dashboard being cluttered with random messages (that's Gmail's job).

I hope you all enjoy utilizing my application and feel free to reach out to me with any questions regarding this application. Happy Job Hunting!
